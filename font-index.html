NO "CHILLING" HERE, WE WATCH THE 'FLIX TO GET INSPIRED

Mood boards for inspiration have been a staple in the
photography industry for decades. Nowadays, we have
so much visual inspiration at our fingertips. We took the
liberty to start your go-to list for visually tantalizing shows
on Netflix.

THE VISUAL STRATOSPHERE OF

NETFLIX

STRANGER THINGS

The sci-fi smash hit's solid cinematography, the work of Tim Ives is perhaps
the key ingredient in establishing the Stranger Things' overall tone, one which
captures that unmissable 80's nostalgia but in a modern and vibrant way. Ives
hits the nail on the head in offering a depiction of suburban life that's true to it's
time--aside from the monsters and parallel worlds--in a relatively lo-fi quality,
but withstyle and color that only today's technological advancements could
allow. Be it the Byer's dullish house entwined in radiant Christmas lights or
the local arcade with it's striking mix of color, Stranger Things' cinematography
allows for an accurate, thoroughly enjoyable decade piece that's in no way
lacking in modernity.
